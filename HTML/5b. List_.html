Sakai Jin thoughts

Lord Shimura
My Uncle the Jito he has always cared for me since my father died, part of me hates what I have become and the toll it has taken on his soul. I pray that one day he will understand that his way cannot save us, before circumstances forces one of us to harm the other.
The Ghost
Ever since the forge, the people of our island have been calling me the ghost. It breaks my heart to think that the mongols have reduced our people to putting their faith in folk tales and not the Samurai. Hopefully when we save my uncle, the people's faith in the Samurai will be restored
Khotun Khan
This man is unlike any foe we have come across. Fierce, strong, intelligent, devious but cruel he kills without honour. If the combined might of all the Samurai clans could not stop him what chance do I have.
